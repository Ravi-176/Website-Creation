Types of Landforms

Mountain
a natural upward projection of the earth's surface, higher and steeper than a hill and often having a rocky summit.
Plain
A plain is a broad area of relatively flat land. Plains are one of the major landforms, or types of land, on Earth.
Desert
A desert is a barren area of landscape where little precipitation occurs and, consequently, living conditions are hostile for plant and animal life.
Plateau
A plateau is a flat, elevated landform that rises sharply above the surrounding area on at least one side.
